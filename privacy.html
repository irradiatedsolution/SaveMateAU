Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15 April 2026  ·  Last Updated: 15 April 2026

🔒 SaveMateAU does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All calculations happen entirely on your device.

1. Overview

SaveMateAU ("the App") is developed and operated by Irradiated Solution Applications (ABN 84 517 752 915). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. SaveMateAU is designed to work entirely on your device — no account required, no data sent to our servers.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

SaveMateAU does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Financial data you enter into calculators
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data beyond what Apple provides

3. Data Stored On Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Usage count — number of calculations performed (used for free tier tracking)
  • Purchase status — whether you have purchased SaveMateAU Pro (managed by Apple's StoreKit)
  • Custom subscription entries — any subscriptions you add in the Subscription Audit feature

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. You can clear this data at any time by deleting and reinstalling the app.

4. In-App Purchases

SaveMateAU offers a one-time in-app purchase ("SaveMateAU Pro") processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles payment data.

5. Third-Party Services

SaveMateAU does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Apple may collect certain diagnostic and usage data as part of iOS. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, not ours.

6. Children's Privacy

SaveMateAU is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Your Rights

Since SaveMateAU does not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete. If you have questions about data stored locally on your device, you can manage or delete it by uninstalling the app.
